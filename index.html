Shower Power

Given current climate issues in the world, Shower Power is an interactive app that encourages users to think about conserving water and energy while they shower. Shower Power will time and promote the shortening of showers. Each successful shower that is finished within the given timeframe will hatch a mystery fish which users can add to their aquarium.

Over the course of three months, Alyssa and I went through the ideation and prototyping/desiging sprints for this project. This is the first time I have gone through the product development cycle and see one of my ideas come to life. Going through user research, information architecture and designing every single fish was a rewarding experience. We are still currently working hard on "bringing this out of the water" (pun intended) on app stores!

Spotify Decades Project

As avid music listeners, my group and I were curious as to how music trends and attributes have changed over the years. Using Spotify's API, we hoped to be able to find and visualize any changes that have occurred in the music industry over mainly the last two decades. Spotify Wrapped gives individual users a look at how they've listened to music via Spotify services, but we wanted to give a holistic analysis of the songs, among qualities like valence, tempo, acousticness, etc., in Spotify's API. Our Research Question is: Observing the top artists of the past two decades, how have music trends and characteristics changed over the years? For example, is there a clear relation between energy and loudness of songs in each decade? And does that relationship stay true across the decades?

Utilizing Jupyter notebook, Pandas and matplotlib, we were able to analyze the 2000 songs we scraped.
